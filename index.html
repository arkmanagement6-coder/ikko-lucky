About Ikko Digital Shoots

Elite Photography & Cinematic Storytellers

Ikko Digital Shoots is a premium photography and videography studio based in Indore. We specialize in high-resolution candid shots, traditional portraits, and cinematic 4K video storytelling that captures the authentic emotions of your milestones.

Common Enquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to common questions about our booking timelines, travel arrangements, photo deliveries, and raw files.

How early should we book our photography session?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for birthdays and corporate events, and 2-6 months in advance for weddings and pre-wedding shoots to ensure our team is available for your dates.

Do you travel outside Indore for destination shoots?

Yes, absolutely! We travel pan-India for destination weddings, pre-weddings, and large events. Travel and accommodation fees are customized based on the destination.

How long does it take to deliver the photos and videos?

We deliver a gorgeous digital sneak-peek folder of 15-20 photos within 3 days of your shoot. The complete edited high-resolution digital album and cinematic films are delivered within 15 to 25 business days.

Do we get the raw files, and how are the final photos delivered?

The final professionally edited and color-graded photos are delivered via secure private online galleries (Google Drive/Dropbox). Raw files can also be provided upon request in our premium packages.
